🌟 Born on the internet 🌐 (and raised in San Diego, California), Scoobert Doobert is a multi-instrumentalist 🎸🎷🎹, producer-engineer 🎧🔧, burrito-connoisseur 🌯🤤, and abundant songwriter 🎶✍️.

His first break to hone his music craft came during a years-long stint touring with The Doobie Brothers 🎸🎤 as a guitarist and vocalist 🎵, and opening for Gregg Allman playing with Lara Johnston 🌟. He then started producing with her after getting off the road 🛣️, continuing to turn his attention towards production 🎵, working with artists like Tamtam 🎤.

Launching his project from his bedroom 🛏️, his single, I'm an Idiot 🤪, was added on Spotify’s New Music Friday 🎶. J-pop band CHAI 🇯🇵 heard the song, and Mr. Doobert was soon remixing their work, which became Miracle (Scoobert Doobert Remix) 🎶. The remix led to frequent productions and songwriting collaborations between CHAI and Mr. Doobert including WHOLE, MY DREAM and other Japan-released exclusives 🌟🎤, featured in the NHK drama, 恋せぬふたり 💑, and the blockbuster film, さかなの子 🐟, respectively.

To continue his chopping of wood 🌲 and carrying of water 💧, Mr. Doobert then dropped the LPs Big Hug 🤗, KŌAN 🌌, and Moonlight Beach 🌙. His newest album MÖB 🎵 sets off a 4 part album cycle so strap in 🚀.

Mr. Doobert likewise is a frequent collaborator, recently working with Victor Marc 🎤, Lou Roy 🎸, I Can't Speak French 🤫, TOMMASO 🎵, Sofasound 🛋️, Jamie Drake 🎶, Joshoo 🎸, Swedish Red Elephant 🐘, Bubby Lewis 🎶, and Limón Limón 🍋.

And check out his Love Music More podcast 🎧, new episodes out Tuesdays 📅. Don't miss it! 🎙️✨
